# FariCars Privacy Policy

FariCars does not collect, store or share any personal user data.

The application communicates locally with FariCars hardware to control supported in-car systems. No user account, personal information, analytics or third-party tracking is used.

We do not transfer any data to external servers. All communication happens directly between the app and the hardware device.
